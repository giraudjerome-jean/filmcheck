About FilmCheck

FilmCheck is a reading tool, not a tribunal.

What it does

FilmCheck helps users read a film through the people behind it. It highlights documented legal or public-conduct signals linked to key credited figures.

How it works

The score displayed on FilmCheck is an interpretation layer. It distinguishes convictions, proceedings, accusations and controversies, and is meant to help users understand context at a glance.

Signal types

FilmCheck separates convictions, proceedings, accusations and controversies, and shows them in a simple, readable way.
